## Constraints

- Input PDFs may be scanned images with inconsistent quality (blur, skew, noise).
- Layout complexity: 1–2 columns, headers/footers, sidebars, callouts.
- Content types: text, tables, figures/diagrams, sometimes equations.
- Must support Bengali + English OCR.
- Must produce multiple export formats (JSON/CSV/Markdown/HTML).
- Must produce confidence scores per stage and per extracted unit where feasible.
